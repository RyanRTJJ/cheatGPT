As a child, Sarah felt out of place. She struggled to make friends and often found solace in books about the supernatural. It wasn't until her teenage years that she realized her true calling: she was a witch, and suddenly everything changed. But being a witch wasn't easy. Society didn't accept her, and she was often ostracized and bullied. So when she happened upon a spell that promised to give her the power to make anyone love her, she couldn't resist.

The spell took her love, but it came at a dark cost. Sarah discovered that the love she received was not genuine, but forced. She had become a puppet master, pulling the strings of to change to suit her own desires.

For years, Sarah used her power to manipulate those around her, always thirsting for more. But as time went on, she began to feel a deep emptiness within her. She realized that love and acceptance could never be truly earned.

Desperate , she decided to finally break the curse she had put on herself, Sarah searched for a way to undo her magic. And that's when she discovered the ancient curse. A powerful magic circle promised to lift the curse, but only through the sacrifice of an innocent life.

Sarah wrestled with the decision for weeks, but ultimately her desire for redemption won out. And so, she lured an unwanted soul to her home and performed the ritual...only to watch in horror as the life drained from the innocent person before her. When the curse was lifted and Sarah's power faded away, she was left with a newfound sense of guilt and shame. She had become the monster she had always feared, and there was no going back.

But despite her regrets, Sarah knew that she had to face the consequences of her actions. And so she turned herself in to the authorities, ready to face whatever punishment awaited her. For even in all of her depravity, she knew that she could never truly escape the true horror of what she had done.
