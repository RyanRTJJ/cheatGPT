The ship of aliens stood frozen for a moment, staring up at the sky in confusion. And then new ships landed, and out emerged an entirely different alien race. Each completely new race of aliens seemed peaceful, and they explained to the humans and other aliens why they had come to help. They had detected the original alien invasion and had come to stop it, stopping it before it could do any harm.

The original alien invaders, realizing they were outnumbered, surrendered peacefully. The new alien race helped the humans and the other aliens repair their damaged systems that had been established to prevent any future invasions from occurring.

In the end, the humans and the aliens all learned an important lesson. Sometimes, it takes an unexpected turn of events to bring us together and work towards a common goal. And in the vastness of space, we are all just small birds trying to navigate our way through the unknown.
