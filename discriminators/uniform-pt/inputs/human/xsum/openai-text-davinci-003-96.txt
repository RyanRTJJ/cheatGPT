Mama, who came through Bristol's academy set-up, played against Warriors for their chief promotion rivals in all four of last season's Championship meetings. He scored an impressive 22 tries in 106 games for Bristol, getting his first Premiership try for Worcester when he crossed in the 24-20 home defeat by Harlequins earlier this month. Warriors host Exeter on Sunday, when they will hope to end a run of 10 straight defeats in the Premiership and European Challenge Cup.
