But Hong Kong's security chief Lai Tung-kwok insisted Mr Lam would not be sent back. "There is no legal arrangement for the transfer of a person to the mainland authorities and the Hong Kong government will handle all cases in accordance with the law of Hong Kong," Mr Lai told reporters on Wednesday. He made his comments a day after meeting Chinese officials in Beijing to discuss the case. Under Hong Kong law, Chinese police do not have jurisdiction in Hong Kong, which is governed under the principle of "One Country, Two Systems".
