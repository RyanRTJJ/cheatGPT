Experts attribute the increase to the effects of the pandemic, which has caused job losses in many sectors, including hospitality and the arts. However, some sectors have seen an increase in employment, including health care and some types of retail.

The labor market appears to be in transition, as economic activity resumes across many parts of the country, though at a slower pace, as restrictions are only gradually eased. With a return to normal economic activity, jobless rates in the US may improve in the next couple of months.
