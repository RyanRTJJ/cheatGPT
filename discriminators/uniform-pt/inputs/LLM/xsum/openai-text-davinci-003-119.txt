The police have urged anyone with information to come forward.

They have already made an arrest as part of the investigation.

The footage showed that the officer was getting into her car when the shooting happened. She was struck in the shoulder and stomach and required surgery.

The officer appealed directly to anyone with information to come forward. She said: “Please help me and my colleagues move forward and help bring justice in this case”.

Detective Chief Inspector Gary Reid said: “We cannot allow such acts of violence to destroy our society. A full and thorough investigation is being conducted by detectives and we are appealing for any information”.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Police Service of Northern Ireland on 101 quoting reference number 1453 22/1/21.
