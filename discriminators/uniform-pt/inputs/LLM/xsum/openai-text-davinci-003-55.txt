The report revealed that there were 209 victims of CSE in 2014 and one young person at risk of exploitation - a total of 210.

The report stated that the majority of the victims were girls between the ages of 11 and 18, with the average age being 15.

The report also revealed that of the victims, 70% were white, 13% were Asian and 11% were black.

West Midlands Police has said it is taking a proactive approach to tackling CSE, adding that it has dedicated officers working to identify potential victims, build trust and provide support.
