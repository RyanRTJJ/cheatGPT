Faf Du Plessis, 29, was dropped for the tour opener because of his "struggling starts against pace". He will replace Chris Woakes in the attack, where he has been a long-time favourite. Woakes was dropped for the first Test against New Zealand.

Steven Finn, 28, is the fourth bowler to be named in the England Test squad. The former England captain, he missed the series in India in 2013 when he was suffering from chest fever but has recovered in time for this tour.

James Anderson, who has been bowling in his second Test for the last month, replaces Chris Woakes at third man in the middle order.

James Vince, who was dropped from the 2012 Ashes tour after his Test debut in Sydney was cut short, makes his first England Test appearance in three years for the match against Sri Lanka.
