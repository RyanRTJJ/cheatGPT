McGarry had been travelling in a car with a group of colleagues to a meeting in the region. It is understood that her detention was sparked by a routine security check and that she was released shortly afterwards.

McGarry is a member of the Scottish National Party, a senior opposition party in Scotland's devolved legislature. She was elected to the UK Parliament at the 2015 General Election, representing the historic Glasgow East constituency. Her release was welcomed by the party, with Deputy Leader Stewart Hosie MP saying "we wish Natalie well".
