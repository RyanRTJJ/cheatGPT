The person was taken to Aberdeen Royal Infirmary with minor injuries, according to the Scottish Fire and Rescue Service. They were given a precautionary check up and released. None of the other people on board were injured.

The vessel was laid up in the harbour, which is close to Stonehaven, in August 2017 as part of the regeneration of Johnshaven. The harbour master said it had been tied up in an appropriate way, allowing for the incoming tide.

The cause of the accident has yet to be established. The Marine Accident Investigation Branch has launched an investigation.
