The Guardian reports this week that a new law could see festival-goers banned from wearing masks, or bringing anything that can hide their identity.

In December, French Prime Minister Bernard Cazeneuve called for the ban to be extended to cover "all kinds of costumes, even for Halloween".

Critics claimed the ban was an arbitrary response to the Paris attacks, and claimed it had a chilling effect on free speech.

A ban in Britain would be seen as an extension of the French law, reported the Guardian.
