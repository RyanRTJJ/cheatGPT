Emotional intelligence is a critical factor in the ability to understand the emotions of others and use this knowledge to effectively interact with them. Emotional intelligence affects the ability of people to manage relationships, handle stress and make decisions. Research has shown that people who have higher emotional intelligence are more likely to have better overall mental health, as well as better physical health, than those with lower emotional intelligence. Additionally, people with greater emotional intelligence have been found to have better social skills and communication skills in relationships, which can result in better performance in work and school.
