In his first year as a solo artist, Tyler was featured on the track "Ridin' Yer Friends" from the soundtrack album Future Friends: The Music of Future Friends.[40][41] The song was a high-profile project of Tyler, Future Friends being his first solo effort released on a major label and followed the success of his album Art Angels in 2008. By the third week of sales, it had risen to number eight on the Billboard 200, just missing the top ten. The album was a Top 15 single, reaching #1 on the Radio Songs chart. The song reached #1 on the Billboard Hot 100, the Hot R&B/HipHop Songs chart and a number one run on the Billboard Top R&B/HipHop Albums chart.
