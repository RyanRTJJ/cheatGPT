In the pop world, acts like Spice Girls, Backstreet Boys, and Britney Spears held strong, while boy bands such as *NSYNC and 98 Degrees had a strong presence in the late-90s music scene.

The emergence of hip hop and R&B was reflective of the diversity found in the entire music scene. Artists such as The Notorious B.I.G., Tupac Shakur, and Wu-Tang Clan were major stars in the rap world. Meanwhile, artists like R. Kelly, Jodeci, Boyz II Men, Lauryn Hill and Destiny's Child were dominating the R&B world.

The rock world saw a number of successful acts rise to prominence in the late-90s, including the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Marilyn Manson, Korn, and Foo Fighters. Emo bands such as Blink-182 and Green Day grew in popularity, while the alternative rock of bands like Radiohead, R.E.M., and Pearl Jam saw a resurgence in the industry.
