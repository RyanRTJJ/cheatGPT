As one of the four great ancient capitals of China, the city has a prominent place in the Chinese history, having served as the capital of several dynasties, kingdoms and empires. Nanjing, one of the most populous cities in China, is currently the capital of Jiangsu Province. It is also the second largest commercial center in the East China region, after Shanghai.

Its heavily-invested infrastructure has helped Nanjing become an important transportation center and shipping port. The city's most prominent landmark is the Nanjing Yangtze River Bridge, the first Yangtze River bridge built by modern engineering methods. Its strategic location near the Yangtze River Delta economic zone makes the city prosper even more.

Nanjing is also a cultural metropolis known for its universities and research institutes, as well as a touristic and shopping center on the Yangtze River Delta. It has numerous historical sites, and its many parks feature spectacular scenery. It is home to some of the most renowned Chinese museums including the Nanjing Museum, the Memorial Hall of the Victims in Nanjing Massacre and the China Modern City Museum. The city is also known for its vibrant nightlife and its many bars, clubs, and lounges.
