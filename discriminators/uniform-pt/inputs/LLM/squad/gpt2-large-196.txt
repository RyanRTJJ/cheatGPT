Queen was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1999 and received a special induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2003. Queen received a Grammy Award in 2004 for Best Performance by a Duo or Group with Additional Percussion on "Bohemian Rhapsody": "Let's Dance (We Will Rock You)" won the Grammy Award for Best Rock Performance and Best Female Vocal Performance and won many other awards as well, including Favorite Metal Performance by a Duo or Group and Female Vocal Performance by a Duo or Group.

In 2009, Queen's "Bohemian Rhapsody" was ranked #1 on Kerrang! and received a Grammy Award for Best Rock Song. In 2013 on the show, "The Queen" performed for over an hour for the first time, ending with Prince's "Let's Go Crazy", and ended the performance by saying:

"I am in awe of how far I have come since you started writing those beautiful words 30 years ago".
