Topping the Hot R&B Songs chart in April 2004, the song entered the Billboard 200 at number 16 and hit number 7 on the Hot R&B Songs chart. The subsequent single, "Hanging on the Telephone," was also issued in February 2005, hitting number nine on the Hot R&B Songs chart. As a result, the song broke into the Hot 100's top 40 and entered the chart's top five prior to being re-released two years later. The remix of the first single, "Hanging On the Telephone" featuring T.I. and Biggie Smalls, peaked at number 13 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart in July 2005.

In July of the same year, the song "I'm a Slave 4 U," featuring Nas and Kanye West, was released by Roc-A-Fella/Interscope.[1] The single entered the Billboard 200 at number 33 and hit number two on the Hot 100. It would remain the only rap single to earn both a Hot 100 and Hot Rap Songs chart number one. As of July 29, 2011 it reached #3 on the U.S. Billboard 200 and number 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
