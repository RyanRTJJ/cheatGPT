He initially started weight training as a way to stay in shape for soccer and other sports. However, after competing in a bodybuilding competition and experiencing the thrill of lifting heavier and heavier weights, Schwarzenegger was hooked. As he devoted more and more time to lifting weights, his grades began to slip and his father made him quit soccer.

Schwarzenegger competed in his first major bodybuilding competition, the Mr. Universe contest, at age 20. He went on to compete in and win several other competitions, including the Mr. World, Mr. Olympia and Mr. Universe contests multiple times each. Schwarzenegger's ambition, effort and large physique quickly earned him fame and respect in the bodybuilding world.
