"Breastfeeding is a natural process for newborns of both sexes and is recommended by the American Academy of Pediatrics, American Academy of Family Physicians, and the American Cancer Society as the most suitable method of food intake until a child is able to independently feed himself or herself. Mothers should continue to breastfeed their infants from as soon as possible, and may reduce the frequency of breastfeeding until the child can express milk. Some individuals in various populations who are not breastfeeding may not have the same health benefits from breastfeeding, however, and some women who breastfeed may not be adequately nourishing their infants, especially those with limited nutrition.
