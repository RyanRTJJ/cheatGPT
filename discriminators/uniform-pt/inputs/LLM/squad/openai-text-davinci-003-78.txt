These bands were also joined by contemporary bands, such as Bon Jovi, Guns N' Roses, and Def Leppard, who revitalized hard rock and heavy metal. All of these bands together helped to create what is known as the "glam-metal" or "hair metal" sound of the mid- to late-1980s. 

All of the bands' resurgences in the mid-1980s helped to change the course of music even today. Classic rock groups such as Deep Purple and Lynyrd Skynyrd were able to bring their difficult-to-replicate sound full circle while still emphasizing its relevance in the sound of 80s music.
