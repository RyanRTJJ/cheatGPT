 United Monarchy, the Israelites were ruled by a number of foreign powers, including the Neo-Assyrian Empire, the Babylonians, the Persian Achaemenid Empire, the Seleucid Empire, the Ptolemaic Kingdom, and the Roman Empire. During this period, Jewish religious autonomy and national identity were preserved in the end by the Jewish diaspora and Hasmonean dynasty, who not only conquered large parts of the Kingdom of Israel, but also formed a powerful Jewish State during the second century BC.
