The Master Sword is a powerful legendary blade that holds great spiritual power and can repel dark magic and evil. Link must use this sacred weapon to fight against the dark forces of Ganon and save Hyrule. When used in conjunction with the Shadow Crystal, Link can transform into a wolf and gain the many advantages that come with it, including heightened senses and the ability to talk with animals. Along his quest, Link will also gain a multitude of new allies, such as the spirits of light that reside within the ancient temples scattered throughout the land. With their help and the combined might of the Master Sword and the Shadow Crystal, Link can face Ganon and break the curse that has taken hold of Hyrule.
