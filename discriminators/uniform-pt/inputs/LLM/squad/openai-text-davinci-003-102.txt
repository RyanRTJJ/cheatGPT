From June 2015 to June 2016, Beyoncé earned an estimated $54 million. From June 2016 to June 2017, Beyoncé earned an estimated $105 million.

In June 2019, Beyoncé earnings were estimated at $81 million, making her the second-highest-earning woman in music behind Taylor Swift.
