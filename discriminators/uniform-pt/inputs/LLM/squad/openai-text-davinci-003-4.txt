In addition, the teams' starters play limited minutes in the NFL preseason, with most backups and undrafted rookies playing more. This ensures that the regular season will not be influenced by any early season injuries that may occur during the preseason. Lastly, the opponents in the preseason games usually consist of other teams within the same conference, so that teams will be able to get a better gauge of their opponents before the start of the regular season.
