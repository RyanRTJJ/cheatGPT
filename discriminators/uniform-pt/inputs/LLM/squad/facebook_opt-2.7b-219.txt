According to the Forbes magazine, Beyoncé's album,Beyonce, has sold more than 10.2 million copies to date in the United States and the United Kingdom alone, with its title track having sold over 3 million copies in the United States alone.In December 2006 the album received 7 Grammy awards nominations and won 5, making it the most successful album of the year in 2006 in The United States among pop albums. The album's theme, On to the Next One, is a line from a song titled "You Are Not Alone" performed by Beyoncé and featured on the film, The Dreamers.The song was also a number-one hit on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 and also sold over two million copies in the United States through January 2010, and was certified 3x-platinum. During July 2010, her single "Don't Hurt Yourself" was the top song on mainstream radio in the United States and the United Kingdom, and debuted at number 23 on the Billboard Hot 100. The song debuted at number one on the British singles chart, making it Beyoncé's third number-one single there to date, following "Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)". The single's accompanying music video, which was her most widely viewed clip of the year, garnered 28 million YouTube views in total.The music video for "Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)", written by Jay-Z and produced by Kanye West, which opens with Beyoncé with her back to the camera showing footage from the opening song (with lyrics reading, "Single ladies, we can't do our own thing, We gotta always, always be, doing everything she said we could"), was Beyoncé's first music video which received positive reviews from fans and critics.
