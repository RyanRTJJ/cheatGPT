The Danaans were the Greeks, who were led by the hero Achilles during the Trojan War. They are known for their courage, skill in battle, and powerful alliance. They included the Abantes, Achaeans, Aeolians, Arcadians, and the Myrmidons. 

The Argives refer to the Trojans, who were led by the ill-fated prince Paris. Their army included the Dardanians, Amazons, and other allies. They were well known for their bravery, but ultimately suffered a great defeat at the hands of the Danaans.
