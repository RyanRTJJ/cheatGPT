On January 8, 2007, he released his major label debut, The Documentary which featured productions from K. Dot and others.

After receiving a Grammy-nomination for Best Rap Album for The Documentary, Tha Crossroads was announced to be his first album under his own label, G-Unit Records, in mid-2010. The album peaked at number 38 on the Billboard 200, while Tha Crossroads was honored as the best rap album in the 2008 Grammy Awards. The album has sold over 500,000 copies in the United States according to Nielsen SoundScan.

On May 9, 2014, Tha Crossroads III was announced to be released on August 6. The album is being produced by DJ Mustard and features several guest appearances produced by Aesop Rock.
