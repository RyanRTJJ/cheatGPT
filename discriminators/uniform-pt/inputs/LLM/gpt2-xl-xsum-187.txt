 Josh Cogger moved to Yorkshire in 2009 and had played for Yorkshire and Yorkshire County. His achievements include being part of the England squad that won the World T20 in 2013. Before his international career, Cogger was a regular in Yorkshire's county cricket, and was also part of the Yorkshire team that won the NatWest T20 Blast last July.
