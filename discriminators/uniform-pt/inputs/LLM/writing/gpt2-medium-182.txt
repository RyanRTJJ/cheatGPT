If not, see <http://www.gnu.org/licenses/>. -------------- An adventure with your favorite robot! This is the story of P-47 "Nuw" and the mysterious and mysterious robot NIMH. I wrote the first part of this story around 1999 or 2000. It was written a few weeks before the release of the robot in the game "Ninja Planet" - a good friend of mine, and a fellow programmer of mine, had asked me to write a story that dealt with some of the robot's adventures and how they lead into the game. I remember thinking that doing just the kind of story I wanted would be too much work as the writer of the game (which was very exciting!). So, I set out writing some stuff with the robots. And then, of course, to write some more. The game and some of the stories actually have parts that are based on my original story, but mostly they are new ideas and characters and the robots have always been the story I remember. But some things had to stay the same even though a lot has changed. There have been lots of revisions here and there.
