
Greetings to the people of Earth! As the leader of a superior species, I come to you today with a very special offer. We have watched the evolution of your species for many years now, and we are amazed at how far you have come. We believe that we can help you develop even further, so that one day you will take your rightful place in the universe.

That is why I come to offer you the gift of knowledge. We can mentor you in the ways of science and technology, to ensure that you reach further than you ever dreamed. Our species has lived in harmony with the cosmos for many ages, and we believe that we can help your species join us in this harmony.

We do not come to force our way of life onto you. Your own culture and way of life is already rich and diverse, and that is something that we both value. We wish to work together, to help you reach your potential and become more than you ever thought possible.

I invite you to open a dialogue with us, and consider our offer to mentor you. We look forward to working with you, and helping you take a giant leap forward in the journey towards a brighter future in the universe.
